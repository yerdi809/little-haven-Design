Why Air Quality Matters in a Nursery

Babies breathe twice as fast as adults and spend up to 16 hours a day sleeping. The quality of the air in their nursery has a direct impact on their health, comfort, and development.

01

Reduce Allergens

Dust, pet dander, pollen, and mold spores are invisible threats. A HEPA air purifier removes up to 99.97% of these particles from your nursery air.

02

Ideal Humidity

The ideal nursery humidity is between 40–60%. Too dry causes congestion and dry skin. A quality humidifier keeps the balance perfect year-round.

03

Right Temperature

The ideal nursery temperature is 68–72°F. A purifier with fan functionality helps you maintain this while keeping air clean and circulating.
