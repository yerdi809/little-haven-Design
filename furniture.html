What to Look for in Nursery Furniture

When choosing nursery furniture, prioritize safety, durability, and comfort. Look for sturdy materials, smooth finishes, and furniture designed specifically for nursery environments. Pieces like gliders, dressers, and changing tables should combine functionality with calming design to support both parents and babies during daily routines.
