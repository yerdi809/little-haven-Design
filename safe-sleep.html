Why Safe Sleep Matters

The first months of your baby's life are the most important for establishing safe, healthy sleep habits. The right environment — and the right products — make all the difference.

01

Firm Sleep Surface

Babies need a firm, flat surface to sleep safely. Soft surfaces increase the risk of suffocation — always choose a certified firm mattress.

02

No Loose Bedding

Keep the crib clear of pillows, blankets, and bumpers. A fitted sheet on a firm mattress is all your baby needs.

03

Right Temperature

The ideal nursery temperature is between 68–72°F. A sleep sack replaces loose blankets and keeps your baby at the perfect temperature all night.
